 About me


My name is José Carlos and for over a decade I worked in advertising and marketing outside of Portugal. I returned in 2016 and for 3 years I was a content manager in the tourism sector.

I have been a father since 2017!!!

In 2020, with the appearance of COVID-19 and all associated restrictions, I decided to take advantage of the confinement and enrolled in a programming course to develop my skills. I have a great fascination for computer science and until then, I had worked mainly with software for creating/editing/treating images, text, 3D models, and rendering.

I started my academic career in the field of Computer Science with a Distributed Systems Programming for Web Environment Course, ★★★★★! In the same year, I started attending the Introduction to TPSI Course and later the CET TPSI (Technological Specialization Course - Specialist Technician in Information Systems Technologies and Programming) from which I lack the Practical Training Module in Work Context.
